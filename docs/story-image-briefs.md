## Art Direction (lock this first — all 45 images must share one style)

**Style:** Modern flat illustration. Clean geometric shapes, bold outlines,
limited palette (deep green, white, navy, ochre/gold, red accents), minimal
gradients, soft drop shadows. Think Apple vector art meets cricket scorecard
aesthetic. Reads clearly at 200×200 px on a phone screen.

**No faces.** Every human figure is helmet-from-side, helmet-from-back,
cap-brim shadow, or fully silhouetted. Umpires wear a white sunhat from
side angle. This is non-negotiable for legal and consistency reasons.

**Aspect ratio:** 1:1 square. Transparent or plain mid-green field background.

**Shared prompt suffix (append to every prompt):**
> *Modern flat illustration, limited palette (deep green, white, navy, ochre),
> bold geometric shapes, minimal gradients, soft drop shadow, 1:1 square,
> plain green field background, no visible faces.*
