### Observations
- **Good length off stump** is the most dangerous zone — 6 batsmen have it as a weakness. This is realistic: off stump bowling is the bread and butter of cricket, and weak batsmen get found out there.
- **Bishnoi on leg stump** triggers zero weaknesses — makes sense for a Bronze bowler. He can still be useful through his spin adjective downgrading neutrals.
- **Bumrah on full middle stump** only directly triggers 2 batsmen's weaknesses — but he's Elite because his seam adjective downgrades everyone else who ISN'T resistant to seam.
- **Arshdeep targeting 5th stump** gets Kohli and Samson — two of the best batters. High risk, high reward bowling.
